Who Is Tack?

Tack is a cobbler somewhere presumibly in the middle-east back in the 17-18th centurey. He falls in love with Princess Yum Yum who he meets after accidentily ruining/stopping the grand parade through the city. He is a shy man who is soft spoken and genuine.

Tack's Characteristics

  1. He is very pale
  2. He is quiet
  3. He is brave
  4. He is a little awkward at times
  5. He is an over-all good guy
